Step-By-Step Checklist

Check each item as you finish it. This checklist stays saved even if you refresh the page.

  1. Ask Stefani for Meta admin access.
    Send: "Please add me to The Reach Meta business account and give me access to the Facebook Page and the linked Instagram account."
  2. Go to Meta Business settings.
    Open business.facebook.com/settings. Confirm you can see The Reach Facebook Page and The Reach Instagram account. If you cannot see both, ask Stefani to update your access.
  3. Collect Facebook Page details.
    Copy the official Facebook Page URL and the Facebook Page ID. Paste them in the form below.
  4. Collect Instagram Business Account ID.
    Copy the Instagram @handle and the Instagram Business Account ID. Paste them in the form below.
  5. Ask Stefani for LinkedIn Page admin access.
    Send: "Please add me as an admin on The Reach LinkedIn Company Page."
  6. Collect LinkedIn Organization ID.
    Copy the LinkedIn Company Page URL and Organization ID. The Organization ID is usually a number. Paste both in the form below.
  7. Save this form.
    Fill the boxes below, press Save + Download TXT, and send the file named THE_REACH_SOCIAL_ACCESS_HANDOFF.txt.
