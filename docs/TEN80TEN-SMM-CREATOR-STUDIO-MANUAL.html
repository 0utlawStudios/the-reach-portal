01What Creator Studio is, in one paragraph

Creator Studio is a spreadsheet inside your Ten80Ten Content Engine where you describe one post per row — date, platform, format, mood, visual style, topic — and click Generate. The AI writes the caption and renders the images (single, carousel, story, or video storyboard), then drops the finished draft directly into the Awaiting Approval column of your kanban. A human reviews and approves. The AI never publishes, schedules, approves, or deletes anything — that's the iron law of this product and Creator Studio respects it absolutely.

🎯

The big idea: you describe the intent in plain language, the AI does the typing and the image rendering, and a human still owns every approve/publish decision. You stay in control. The AI gives you a faster first draft, not a finished post.

06The auto-revise loop

This is the most powerful feature, and the one most likely to feel like magic. Workflow:

  1. Open an AI-generated card and decide what's wrong.

    Maybe slide 3 is too text-heavy, the hook is generic, or the CTA missed the brief.

  2. Drag the card to "Revision Needed".

    This works for AI posts and manual posts. For manual posts, your existing kickback flow runs. For AI posts, Creator Studio kicks in.

  3. Write reviewer notes (≥10 characters).

    Be specific. "Slide 3 has too much copy — cut to one bold sentence. Hook should lead with a time-cost, not a generic question."

  4. Save.

    Don't click anything else. The Supabase Database Webhook fires, the worker enqueues a revise job, and within about 30 seconds the card moves itself back to Awaiting Approval with the revised content. The revision counter shows v2, then v3, etc.

🔁

Why it works without clicks: Supabase's database webhook fires on every posts UPDATE. Our handler filters for the exact transition — AI post, stage flipping to revision_needed, notes ≥10 chars — and enqueues a revise job. Anything that doesn't match returns 204 and nothing happens.

08Hallucination guardrails — what the AI cannot do

This is a creator tool for a real business. Fabricated facts in your captions would be costly. Every draft passes a three-stage gate before it's allowed into Awaiting Approval. If the gate fails, the AI regenerates once with the violations pinned in the prompt. If the second attempt also fails, the job ends as Failed with a clear reason — no half-baked post is ever inserted.

Stage 1 — Regex sweep

The following are automatic kills unless your input row literally contained that exact text:

  • Any percentage: 73%, 45.5 %
  • Any dollar amount: $10K, $1,200, $5M
  • Phrases: "studies show", "research proves", "data shows", "experts agree", "according to a study", "X out of Y"
  • Year strings outside the current year ±1

Stage 2 — Trusted-corpus cross-reference

The gate builds a corpus from your row inputs plus the brand_playbook (tagline, voice, hashtags, hooks, CTAs, content pillars, focus list, avoid list). Anything specific in the caption that doesn't appear in the corpus is flagged.

Stage 3 — Second-LLM verifier

If the regex sweep is clean, a fast pass through gpt-4o-mini asks: "list any specific claims here that are not in the input corpus." Empty list passes. Anything else fails.

💡

If the gate trips on a legitimate fact (e.g. you really do have a $10K MRR client and want to mention it), put the fact in the Notes column of the plan row. The gate sees it in the corpus and lets it through.

11Troubleshooting

⚠️

"Fill in the Brand Kit before generating AI drafts."

Open Brand Kit from the sidebar. Fill at minimum: tagline, brand voice, content pillars, hashtag core, hooks (3–5), CTAs (3–5). Save. The placeholder detector trips on Sample hook 1, Define your brand voice, or the literal word placeholder — replace those and the gate passes.

⏱️

Row stuck on "Generating…" for more than 5 minutes.

The cron worker reclaims jobs stuck in running for >5 minutes. Wait one cron cycle and the job auto-retries. If it stays stuck, click Cancel — the row flips back to Ready and you can re-Generate.

🛑

Job ended with hallucination_gate_failed.

Read the violation list in Last Error. Common causes: you mentioned a stat in Topic but not in Notes, or the model invented a customer name. Add the legit fact to Notes, or tighten Style Prompt with "do not name specific customers."

💰

Daily cap hit before noon.

Bump OPENAI_DAILY_CAP_USD in Vercel env vars (Production scope) and redeploy. Or reduce slides per carousel — the image bill scales linearly.

🔌

Auto-revise didn't fire when I dragged a card to Revision Needed.

Check three things:

  1. SUPABASE_WEBHOOK_SECRET in Vercel matches the one in the Supabase Database Webhook header byte-for-byte.
  2. The card has generated_by_model set (i.e. it was AI-generated, not manual).
  3. Reviewer notes are at least 10 characters. Shorter notes are filtered as accidental drag.

12A full worked example

Goal: A LinkedIn carousel about "Why founders should automate hiring intake."

  1. Open Creator Studio

    Sidebar → Sparkles icon.

  2. Fill row 1

    Date 2026-05-15 · Time 09:00 · Platforms LinkedIn · Media Image · Format Carousel · Slides 5 · Feel Educational · Visual Style Infographic · Style Prompt "clean white background, single orange #FF6A00 accent, bold sans-serif, no people, slide number top-right" · Topic "Why founders should automate their hiring intake process" · Notes "Address the founder pain of repetitive interviews. End with CTA: book a 15-min systems audit."

    Aspect chip auto-updates to 4:5 · 1080×1350. Status flips from Empty to Ready. Generate button enables.

  3. Click Generate

    Status → Generating. Toast: "AI generation started — back in about 20 seconds."

  4. Wait ≈60 seconds

    Carousels take longer than single images because each slide is a separate image call. Status → Generated. Toast: "AI draft ready — check Awaiting Approval."

  5. Open the card

    5 carousel images in correct 4:5 dimensions, a hook-led caption, hashtags mixing your brand core with topical tags, the purple AI panel with quality score (e.g. 7/10) and the AI's approval note explaining intent.

  6. Reviewer pass

    You decide slide 3 is too text-heavy. Drag to Revision Needed. Note: "Slide 3 — cut to one bold sentence. Hook is weak, lead with a specific time-cost." Save.

  7. Wait ≈30 seconds

    Card animates back to Awaiting Approval. Slide 3 is now one bold line. Hook references the time-cost. Revision counter: v2.

  8. Approve manually

    Drag to Approved / Scheduled. Confirm date/time. n8n picks it up for publishing on the scheduled timestamp.

Total cost ≈ $2.50 generation + $2.00 revision = $4.50 from your $10 daily cap.
