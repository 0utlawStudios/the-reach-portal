# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Ten80Ten and The Reach social media operators, approvers, creative directors, and admins use this app during daily content planning, review, approval, scheduling, publishing, and support workflows.

## Product Purpose
The Reach SMM Portal is an authenticated content operations tool for managing posts from idea through approval and posting, with media assets, comments, revisions, team access, and auditability. Success means users can trust the board as the source of truth, see their work quickly after refresh, and never lose posts silently.

## Brand Personality
Focused, reliable, operational. The interface should feel quiet, fast, and precise: a workbench for repeated use, not a marketing presentation.

## Anti-references
Avoid landing-page composition, decorative motion, oversized hero styling, playful widgets that obscure task flow, ambiguous destructive actions, and any UI pattern that makes data status unclear.

## Design Principles
- Make content state unmistakable: stage, approval status, media, and ownership should be easy to scan.
- Preserve trust over flourish: data safety, clear errors, and auditability beat decorative UI.
- Optimize for repeated operations: common content workflows should be dense, direct, and fast.
- Keep motion purposeful: use animation to teach state or draw attention to a control, never to delay work.
- Treat performance as part of usability: refresh should show a useful shell immediately and avoid redundant startup work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Use product-grade accessible defaults: keyboard-reachable controls, visible focus, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion alternatives for animation, and clear text labels or tooltips for icon-only controls.
